Prevent being from fall down

Once the baby reaches the age of can crawl and walk, parent need to pay full attention to baby. The baby won't know what the danger is, they just crawl and walk around. In addition, children like to jump on the couch. Children can fall from stairs and chairs without their parents noticing . Not only can it hurt the head, but it can also cause fractures.

Don't climb up the stair railiing.

What should we do when child fall down?

Prevent from getting burnt

Children can easily get burned out of curiosity. Because they can't reach the table but want to see what's on it, they pull things off the table to see. If hot water or hot objects are placed on the table, the child may be scalded by the hot water . Not only that,the child will pull the pot off the stove, touch the iron, and put his hand in the hot water . This is how children get burned.



Here are some Do and Don't

Get Burn

Don't pull all the things near you.
Don't play in the Kitchen

Get Burn

Don't touch the iron.

What can happen？

Can get burn and fatal injury.

What can happen?



Some burns may last forever

How can we prevent

Keep hot objects as far away from children as possible

How can we prevent
