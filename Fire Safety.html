How do we safe byself In Fire

If you heard Fire Alram or Somke Alarm:

01
Don't Be Scared.

If you start to get scared or nervous,
your mind will be completely
overwhelmed by your nervousness and you
won't know what to do in the fire. You just
stood there and got burned by the fire.

The correct approach is:

don't be afraid. Stay Alarm your mind.
Then think about how to get out of the fire,
how to get to the exit, how to save yourself.

Fear and nervousness will not save you, they will only hurt you

02
Don't Hide,
Go Outside

If you notice that your house or buliding catch fire:

Don't hide in the room or house.
Smoke can come in from small gaps,
and over time it will become a lot, causing
the room to be filled with smoke,
choked by smoke, and unable to breathe

The correct approach is:

If the fire is not too big, take important
objects or items.Then make sure there's no
one else in the house and go outside.

Before opening the door,
make sure the door is hot or not,
can touch or not.

If you have a situation where you cannot get
out (example: trapped in a room, disabled legs),
open a window to let others know you are still inside
or ask someone for help with shout.

03
Call 911

Call 911 as soon as you know it's on fire.
Only professional and specially trained
personnel can save people and put out
fires without injury.

Speak calmly when calling 911. Need to
tell exactly where the fire is, how big it is.
Then wait quietly for the firefighters to arrive

04
Other Safety Tips

If your Clothes caught Fire:

Stop

Drop

Roll

If you are in the full smoke of hallway:

Use wet tower to cover the Mouth & Nose then Crawl
